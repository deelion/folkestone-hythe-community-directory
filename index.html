What is this?

goodneighbours is an attempt to make it easier for fellow Folkestone & Hythe residents to find and engage with the vibrant community organisations across our district. Whether you're looking for fun, free activities, an opportunity to volunteer, or are in need of direct support for your needs, the goal is to make it all available here.

This is a space to highlight the amazing work of our neighbours in the Folkestone & Hythe district through community groups, charities, community interest companies, and other community-focused businesses. district.

Advice
