What is Folke.world?

Folke.world is a space to capture how people in the Folkestone & Hythe district are building community – something ‘greater than the sum of its parts’ – to enable wellbeing, resilience and ‘the good life’ for all that live here.

Visit the Folke.world blog to learn more about Folkestone & Hythe's community network and how we can support it.
