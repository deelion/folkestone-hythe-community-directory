Please note: This site is currently run by one person, gathering information from around the web from available sources - please confirm with the organisations before making plans.

What is this?

goodneighbours is an attempt to make it easier for Folkestone & Hythe residents to find and engage with the incredible network of community organisations across our district. Whether you're looking for fun, free activities, an opportunity to volunteer, or are in need of direct support for your needs, the goal is to make it all easier to find right here.
